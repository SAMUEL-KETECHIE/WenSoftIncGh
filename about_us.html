QUALITY COMMITMENT


WenSoft Quality Policy


WenSoft consistently provides services and products that meet or exceed customer requirements. We empower each employee by providing resources and training to ensure continuous process improvement.
This quality approach is designed to maximize customer satisfaction and employee fulfillment.

Quality Management System


WenSoft’s commitment to quality resulted in the establishment and implementation of a documented Quality Management System compliant with ISO 9001:2008 requirements.

WenSoft maintains this formal Quality Management System, with Executive Management Responsibility, to ensure that products conform to specific contract requirements and comply with the ISO 9001:2008 standard.

Additionally, WenSoft processes and procedures meet the industry-best practices defined in the Capability Maturity Model Integration (CMMI®) for Development, version 1.2.

WenSoft service desk processes and procedures conform to the best practices defined in Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) version 3.
